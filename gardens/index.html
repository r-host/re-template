Welcome to Gardener Landscaping Company

We have a fully trained experienced service department ready to handle all kind of your landscaping service needs. We have been in relocation and landscaping service business since 1990. We provide ongoing property maintenance which includes lawn mowing, fertilizing, spring and fall cleanups, de-thatching, aerating, seeding, mulching, edging, herbicide application, plant health care, snow plowing, pruning, and tree care and removal.

We also provide the design and installation of many landscape construction projects including walkways, steps, retaining walls, patios, veneer stone,planting, trans-planting, drainage systems, lawn renovation and installation, paver driveways, cobblestone edging, and excavation.
